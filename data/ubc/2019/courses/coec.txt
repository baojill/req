

code: COEC 126
name: Data: Description, Display, and Inference
cred: 3
desc: Use of data and statistics to convey information on important issues of the world economy. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 126 or COMM 126. 

code: COEC 293
name: Financial Accounting
cred: 3
desc: Construction and interpretation of financial reports prepared primarily for external use. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 293 or COMM 293. 

code: COEC 294
name: Managerial Accounting
cred: 3
desc: Introduction to the development and use of accounting information for management planning and control and the development of cost information for financial reports. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 294 or COMM 294. 
prer: One of COEC 293, COMM 293.
preq: COEC 293 or COMM 293

code: COEC 298
name: Introduction to Finance
cred: 3
desc: Basic principles of financial valuation, including the time-value of money and the risk/return tradeoff. Develops tools for the quantitative analysis of corporate and/or individual financing and saving decisions, and of capital budgeting decisions. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 293 or COMM 298. 
prer: All of COEC 293, ECON 204.
preq: COEC 293 and ECON 204

code: COEC 365
name: Market Research
cred: 3
desc: The process of marketing research including problem/opportunity formulation, research objectives, data sources, research instrument design, sampling, data collection and processing and methods of data analysis. 
prer: Either (a) all of ECON 315, ECON 316 or (b) ECON 308.
preq: (ECON 315 and ECON 316) or ECON 308

code: COEC 370
name: Corporate Finance
cred: 3
desc: Corporate enterprise decisions including capital budgeting; capital structure choice; and financial policies, tools, and valuation. 
prer: Either (a) all of ECON 315, ECON 316 or (b) ECON 308.
preq: (ECON 315 and ECON 316) or ECON 308

code: COEC 371
name: Investment Theory
cred: 3
desc: Basic concepts of finance, including security valuation, security markets, and financial decisions concerning risk and return. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 371 or COMM 371. 
prer: Either (a) ECON 308 or (b) all of ECON 315, ECON 316.
preq: ECON 308 or (ECON 315 and ECON 316)

code: COEC 377
name: International Financial Markets and Institutions
cred: 3
desc: Structure, nature and institutions of foreign exchange markets, including spot, forward, futures, options, and offshore currency markets. Factors affecting exchange rates are also discussed. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 377 or COMM 377. 
prer: COEC 371.
preq: COEC 371

code: COEC 394
name: Government and Business
cred: 3
desc: Roles of government and business in the Canadian economy including effects of public policy on the business environment. Ethical foundations of government, business and personal decision-making. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 394 or COMM 394. 
prer: ECON 204.
preq: ECON 204

code: COEC 437
name: Database Technology
cred: 3
desc: Theory and technology of database management from an applications perspective; database design; database administration. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 437, COMM 437, or CPSC 304. 

code: COEC 475
name: The Economics & Policy of the Environment, Energy, and Natural Resources
cred: 3
desc: Economic analysis of environmental problems, energy systems, and natural resources. Policy options to help resolve these issues. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 475 or COMM 487. 
prer: ECON 315 and one of ECON 316, ECON 308.
preq: ECON 315 and (ECON 316 or ECON 308)

code: COEC 491
name: Strategic Management
cred: 3
desc: Concepts and processes for the strategic management of single- and multi-business unit private sector enterprises, analyzed using the case method. Integrates methodologies from economic and organizational theory to form the foundations for strategic analyses. 

code: COEC 497
name: New Enterprise Development
cred: 3
desc: The particular problems and experiences encountered in starting, developing, and managing new enterprises. The course includes lectures, guest speakers, and case studies. 

code: COEC 498
name: International Business Management
cred: 3
desc: Development of general environmental framework for international business studies by drawing on international and development economics, research into government-business relations and studies in comparative socio-cultural systems and political systems. Credit will be granted for only one of COEC 498 or COMM 498.
