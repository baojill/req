code: ECON 472
name: Economics of Renewable Resources
cred: 3
desc: Application of economic analysis to the management of renewable resources. Special attention is given to criteria for the optimal use of depleting resources such as forests and water. prer: One of ECON 301, ECON 304.
preq: ECON 301 or ECON 304

code: ECON 487
name: Comparative Economic Systems
cred: 3
desc: Economic analysis of non-market/non-price systems of resource allocation. Economic analysis of central planning, co-ordination problems in hierarchical organizations, and the role of quantity restrictions, quotas, standards, etc. in regulating economic behaviour. The Soviet system of economic planning is used throughout as an example of the issues discussed. Credit may be obtained for only one of ECON 387 or ECON 487. 
prer: One of ECON 301, ECON 304.
preq: ECON 301 or ECON 304
